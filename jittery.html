What's New
Jitter just got upgraded
Two big changes to how messaging works — take 30 seconds to read this.
Usernames
Every member now has a unique @username. This is your permanent identity on Jitter — other people search for you by username to add you. It cannot be changed after you set it, so choose carefully.
Friends First
You can no longer message someone out of the blue. You must send a friend request and they have to accept it before a conversation opens. This keeps your inbox clean and your privacy intact.
Your Existing Chats Are Safe
All your previous conversations are still there. You're just choosing a username now so people can find and add you going forward.

Saving Images
Saved to Your Device

Images in X-ORBIT Direct Messages are hosted on our servers and automatically deleted after 14 days to conserve our limited storage space.

Clicking save stores a copy of the image directly on your device in your browser's local storage, so it remains visible in chat even after it has been removed from X-ORBIT servers.

Saved images are stored only on this device and browser. They will not appear if you log in from a different device. You can unsave an image at any time by clicking the checkmark button.
